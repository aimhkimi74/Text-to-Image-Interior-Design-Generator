Help & Inspiration

Everything you need to navigate the world of AI interior styling and design generation. Get started, get inspired.

Account & Login

  • Create your account to start designing.
  • Reset your password if needed via the login page.
  • Profile updates are handled from your dashboard.

Design with AI

  • Describe your dream space using natural language.
  • Use “Style Preference” to guide the result or let AI choose.
  • Each message returns an image with analysis and details.

Understanding Styles

  • Styles supported: Modern, Scandinavian, Industrial, Mid-Century, Traditional, Coastal, and Rustic.
  • Each image response includes a detected style and optional explanation.
  • Mismatch? Suggest your own using the feedback modal.

Rate and Improve

  • Rate images by relevance, quality, and style accuracy.
  • Your ratings help improve results for all users.
  • Submit feedback for style misidentification.

Favorites & History

  • Click the heart icon to save a design.
  • Access your favorited and recent designs in the Profile page.
  • Download any image for inspiration or reference.

Dashboard Insights

  • See your top-rated and most used styles.
  • Track the number of designs you’ve generated.
  • Edit your profile details and preferences anytime.

Submitting Feedback

  • You can provide feedback through the System Usability Scale (SUS) form.
  • To access it, navigate to the Feedback page under your account dashboard or visit the direct feedback link.
  • The form asks you to rate 10 statements on a scale from 1 to 5 and provide optional comments and user role.
  • Once submitted, your SUS score is calculated automatically and shown back to you as confirmation.
  • You may submit updated feedback again after 1 day, if desired.
  • This helps us continuously improve the user experience across all features.

FAQs

Is it free to use?

Yes! All core features are free to access and use. No subscription required.

Can I use the generated images for commercial projects?

Currently, generated designs are for personal use only. Commercial licensing is planned for the future.

How do I recover a forgotten password?

Go to the login page and click on “Forgot Password” to receive a reset code via email.

Can I upload a photo of my own room?

This feature is coming soon! Stay tuned for updates on photo uploads and custom room analysis.

How do I delete or rename a session?

Use the session menu in the sidebar to rename or remove any saved design session.

What styles are supported?

We support a variety of design styles including Modern, Scandinavian, Industrial, Mid-Century, Traditional, Coastal, and Rustic. More are being added regularly.

How can I contact support?

Email us at livingroom.helpxxx@gmail.com and our team will be happy to help.
